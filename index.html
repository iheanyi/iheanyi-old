Projects

Some of my most recent projects that I've been working on.

  • Wabruda I recently was given the honor to re-design the website for Wabruda, an organization focusing on professional development among African-American men.

  • Audite Audite served as my final project for my Programming Paradigms course. Utilizing the CherryPy micro webframework and the EchoNest API, Audite streams back music recommendations back to the user based on an initial artist query.

  • Tubalr For my Mobile Application Projects final, I decided to make a native Android application for Tubalr, an online music streaming application. Tubalr currently has over 5k installs in the Google Play Store, development is halted at the moment due to my undergraduate research and schoolwork.

You can view most of my projects on my GitHub page.
